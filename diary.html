Rizal's own Story of his Life

This is a short autobiography written by José Rizal in the last years of his life. The account details his early years, education, travels, and the development of his nationalistic ideas. Rizal’s narrative sheds light on his motivations and the deep connection he felt to the Filipino people and their struggle for freedom. The autobiography gives a personal perspective on his transformation from a young scholar to a key figure in the Philippine revolution.
